HP/GP/MP Cone Crushers

Metso HP/GP/MP cone crushers are the industry standard for secondary, tertiary and quaternary crushing. The HP series utilizes a higher speed and throw design to increase capacity while producing a tighter product gradation. Available from HP200 to HP800, these crushers process up to 1,200 tph in mining and aggregate applications. Key wear parts include mantles, concaves and bowl liners, where Anranst provides Mn18Cr2 work-hardening steel with 20-30% longer service life than standard Mn13 alternatives.

Why Choose Anranst

  • Optimized chamber profiles matching HP200-HP800 geometry for consistent CSS and product gradation
  • Mn18Cr2+TiC composite option delivers 2-3x longer life in abrasive hard-rock applications (granite, basalt)
  • Precision machining ensures ±0.5mm tolerance for mantle-concave gap control
  • Full model coverage: HP200, HP300, HP400, HP500, HP600, HP700, HP800; GP100-GP550; MP800-MP1250

Maintenance Recommendations

Inspect mantle and concave wear profile every 500 operating hours. Replace when wear reaches 80% of original thickness. Lubrication system oil change every 2,000 hours. Check CSS monthly and adjust as needed.
