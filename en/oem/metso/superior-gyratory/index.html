Superior Gyratory

Metso Superior primary gyratory crushers are designed for the most demanding high-capacity primary crushing applications. With feed openings up to 60 inches, they process run-of-mine ore at rates exceeding 8,000 tph. The spider-arm design with hydraulic positioning allows precise CSS adjustment. Anranst provides mantles and concaves in Mn18Cr2 and premium Mn22Cr2Mo for extended service intervals.
