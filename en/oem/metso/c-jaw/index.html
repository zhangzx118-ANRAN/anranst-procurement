C-Series Jaw Crushers

Metso C-series jaw crushers are engineered for the toughest feed materials. The deep symmetrical crushing chamber with optimized nip angle maximizes feed acceptance and capacity. C-series models from C80 to C200 handle primary crushing in mining and quarrying, with throughput up to 800 tph. Anranst jaw plates in Mn13Cr2, Mn18Cr2 and premium Mn22Cr2Mo provide optimal work-hardening performance matched to your specific application.
