NP Impact Crushers

Metso NP series horizontal shaft impact (HSI) crushers deliver versatile crushing for secondary and tertiary applications. Advanced rotor design with optimized blow bar positioning ensures consistent product quality and high reduction ratios. Available from NP1003 to NP2023, these crushers handle up to 1,800 tph. Anranst supplies blow bars in Mn18Cr2, Cr20 and Cr26 for different feed hardness conditions.
