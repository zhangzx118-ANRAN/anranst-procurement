Metso

Vertimill

Metso Vertimill is the industry leader in vertical stirred grinding technology. Delivering up to 40% energy savings compared to conventional ball mills, Vertimills are ideal for regrinding, secondary grinding and fine grinding applications. The screw agitator provides efficient grinding with minimal media consumption. Anranst provides Vertimill liners and screw segments in Cr26 high-chrome iron and ceramic composite options.
