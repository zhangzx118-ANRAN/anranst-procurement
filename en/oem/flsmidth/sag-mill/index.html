SAG/AG Mills

FLSmidth SAG/AG mills are designed for the world's largest mining operations. With installed power up to 28 MW and mill diameters exceeding 40 feet, these mills set the benchmark for primary grinding performance. FLSmidth's innovative liner designs optimize charge motion for maximum throughput. Anranst supplies complete SAG mill liner packages with lifter bars in Mn18Cr2 and grate panels in Cr26 high-chrome iron.
