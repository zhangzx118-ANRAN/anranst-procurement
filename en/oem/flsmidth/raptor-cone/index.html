Raptor Cone Crushers

FLSmidth Raptor cone crushers set the standard for high-capacity secondary and tertiary crushing. The Raptor series features a larger feed opening and higher pivot point for greater throughput. Models from Raptor XL300 to XL1100 handle capacities up to 2,000 tph. The integrated lubrication and hydraulic system ensures reliable operation. Anranst provides Raptor mantles and bowl liners with verified alloy composition for maximum service life.
