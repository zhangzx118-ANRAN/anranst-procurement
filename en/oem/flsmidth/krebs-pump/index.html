KREBS Slurry Pumps

FLSmidth KREBS millMAX slurry pumps are engineered for the most abrasive and corrosive mining applications. The patented millMAX design reduces recirculation and slurry surging, extending wear life by up to 50%. Available in sizes from 2-inch to 26-inch discharge, these pumps handle mill discharge, cyclone feed and tailings transport. Anranst supplies impellers, liners and throatbushes in Cr26 high-chrome iron and elastomer options.
