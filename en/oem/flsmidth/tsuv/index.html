TSUV Gyratory Crushers

FLSmidth TSUV gyratory crushers represent the next generation of primary crushing technology. With a 30% capacity increase over previous models, the TSUV features an optimized crushing chamber design with 70% more crushing surface area. The modular concave design reduces change-out time by 50%. Anranst provides TSUV mantles and concave segments in Mn22Cr2Mo premium alloy for maximum throughput.
