DRC Double Roll Crushers

FLSmidth DRC (Double Roll Crusher) is designed for primary and secondary crushing of soft to medium-hard materials. The compact design with direct drive system provides efficient size reduction at low operational costs. DRC models feature hydraulic gap adjustment and automatic tramp iron protection. Anranst supplies roll shells and segments in Mn18Cr2 and ZG30CrMnMo alloy steel.
