ThyssenKrupp

Polysius Vertical Mills

ThyssenKrupp Polysius vertical roller mills are the industry standard for cement and slag grinding. The modular design allows flexible configuration with 2, 3 or 4 rollers. The patented Sepax separator and LSKS classifier system achieve precise product fineness control. Anranst supplies grinding rollers, table liners and classifier vanes in Cr26 high-chrome iron with ceramic composite options.
