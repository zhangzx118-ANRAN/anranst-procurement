KB Gyratory Crushers

ThyssenKrupp KB gyratory crushers are designed for primary crushing of run-of-mine ore at the highest capacities. With feed openings up to 1,830 mm, KB series crushers process up to 14,000 tph. The spider-arm design with hydraulic main shaft positioning ensures consistent product quality. Anranst provides KB mantles and concave segments in Mn18Cr2 and premium Mn22Cr2Mo alloy.
