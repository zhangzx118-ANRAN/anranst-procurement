Roll Sizer (CenterSizer & RollSizer DRS)

ThyssenKrupp RollSizer DRS and CenterSizer are specialized for primary and secondary crushing of soft to medium-hard materials. The low-speed twin-shaft design with optimized tooth geometry minimizes fines generation while achieving precise product sizing. Anranst manufactures DRS tooth picks in verified ZG30CrMnMo alloy steel with complete QC documentation and chemical composition certificates.
