ERC Eccentric Roll Crushers

ThyssenKrupp ERC (Eccentric Roll Crusher) combines the advantages of jaw and roll crushers in a single machine. The eccentric roll generates both compression and impact forces for high reduction ratios with a compact design. Processing up to 8,000 tph, the ERC excels in hard-rock primary crushing. Anranst supplies impact bars, crushing segments and roll shells in ZG30CrMnMo and Mn18Cr2 alloy steel.
