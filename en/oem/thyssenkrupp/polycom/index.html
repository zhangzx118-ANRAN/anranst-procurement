POLYCOM HPGR

ThyssenKrupp POLYCOM HPGR is the world's leading high-pressure grinding roll for energy-efficient comminution. Operating at specific pressing forces up to 8.5 N/mm², the POLYCOM achieves size reduction ratios of up to 10:1 in a single pass. The stud-lined roll surface creates an autogenous wear protection layer. Anranst supplies tungsten carbide studs and roll shell segments for POLYCOM maintenance.
