CITIC Heavy Industries

SAG/AG/Ball Mills

CITIC Heavy Industries SAG mills are among the world's largest grinding mills, with diameters up to 12.2 m and installed power exceeding 28 MW. Designed for China's mega-mine operations, CITIC SAG mills deliver high throughput with optimized liner configurations. Anranst supplies complete SAG mill liner packages including lifter bars in Mn18Cr2 and grate panels in Cr26 high-chrome iron.
