CITIC Heavy Industries

Gyratory Crushers

CITIC Heavy Industries gyratory crushers are China's largest primary crushing equipment, designed for mega-mine applications. With feed openings up to 1,800 mm and capacities exceeding 10,000 tph, CITIC gyratories serve China's largest iron ore and copper operations. Anranst provides mantles and concave segments in Mn18Cr2 and premium Mn22Cr2Mo alloy for extended service intervals in Chinese mega-mines.
