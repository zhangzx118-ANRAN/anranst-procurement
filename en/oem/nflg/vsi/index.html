VSI Crushers

NFLG VSI (Vertical Shaft Impact) crushers are engineered for premium sand and aggregate production. The rock-on-rock and rock-on-steel crushing principles produce cubical product shape ideal for concrete and asphalt. NFLG VSI models handle throughputs up to 650 tph. Anranst supplies rotor tips with tungsten carbide inserts, impact plates and distribution cones in Cr20/Cr26 high-chrome iron.
