VSI Sand Makers

SBM VSI vertical shaft impact crushers produce premium manufactured sand and shaped aggregates. The dual-feed system supports both rock-on-rock and rock-on-steel crushing modes. VSI models handle throughputs up to 550 tph for sand and aggregate production. Anranst supplies rotor tips, impact plates and distribution cones in Cr20 and Cr26 high-chrome iron with tungsten carbide insert options.

Typical Applications

Miningquarryingaggregatescementrecycling.
