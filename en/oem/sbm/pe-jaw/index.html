PE Jaw Crushers

SBM PE series jaw crushers provide cost-effective primary crushing for mining and quarrying applications. The deep crushing chamber with optimized nip angle maximizes feed capacity. PE models from PE400×600 to PE1500×1800 handle throughputs up to 1,000 tph. Anranst provides jaw plates in Mn13Cr2 and Mn18Cr2 with OEM-compatible dimensions and competitive pricing.
