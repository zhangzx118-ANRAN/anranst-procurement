HPC Cone Crushers

SBM HPC series hydraulic cone crushers deliver reliable secondary and tertiary crushing performance. The hydraulic adjustment system provides quick CSS setting, while the fixed main shaft design ensures stable operation. HPC models from HPC160 to HPC400 handle capacities up to 700 tph. Anranst provides mantles and concaves in Mn18Cr2 and Mn22Cr2Mo with OEM-compatible quality.
