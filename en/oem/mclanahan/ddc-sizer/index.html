DDC-Sizers

McLanahan DDC-Sizers are twin-shaft direct-drive crushers designed for primary and secondary crushing of coal, salt and soft minerals. The compact design with toothed rolls processes material with precise sizing and minimal fines. DDC-Sizers feature bolt-on tooth segments for rapid maintenance. Anranst provides tooth segments and roll shells in ZG30CrMnMo and ZG30CrNiMo alloy steel.
