McLanahan

Triple/Double Roll Crushers

McLanahan roll crushers are designed for primary and secondary crushing of medium-hard to soft materials. The twin-roll design with direct drive provides efficient size reduction at low operational costs. Available in single-roll, double-roll and quad-roll configurations for different applications. Anranst supplies roll shells, segments and scraper blades in Mn18Cr2 and ZG30CrMnMo alloy steel.
