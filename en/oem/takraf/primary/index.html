TAKRAF

Primary Crushers

Takraf primary gyratory crushers are engineered for the world's largest open-pit mines. With feed openings exceeding 1,800 mm and capacities up to 12,000 tph, these crushers handle the highest throughput requirements. The robust spider and eccentric design ensures reliable operation under extreme conditions. Anranst provides mantles and concave segments in Mn22Cr2Mo premium alloy for maximum service life.
