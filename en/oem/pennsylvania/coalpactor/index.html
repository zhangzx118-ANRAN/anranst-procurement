Pennsylvania Crusher

CoalPactors

Pennsylvania CoalPactors are single-roll impact crushers designed specifically for coal and similar friable materials. The compact design features a heavy-duty roll with paddle hammers that strike material against adjustable breaker plates. CoalPactors achieve high reduction ratios with minimal fines generation. Anranst provides paddle hammers, ring hammers and tooth picks with ASTM-certified materials.

Typical Applications

Coal crushingpetroleum cokesaltsand other friable materials.
