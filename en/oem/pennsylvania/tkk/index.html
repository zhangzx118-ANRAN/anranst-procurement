Pennsylvania Crusher

TKK Granulators

Pennsylvania TKK Granulators are ring-type crushers specifically designed for coal processing. The rotating ring hammers generate impact forces for efficient coal size reduction while the grid structure controls product size. TKK models handle capacities up to 2,000 tph in power plant and coal preparation applications. Anranst provides ring hammers in AISI 4150H forged steel and paddle hammers in ASTM A128 Grade B-2.

Typical Applications

Coal crushing and sizing in power plants and coal preparation facilities.
