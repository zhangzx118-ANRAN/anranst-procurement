МШР Ball Mills

UZTM (УЗТМ) MSHR ball mills are Russia's standard grinding equipment for mineral processing. With diameters from 2.1 m to 5.5 m, MSHR mills serve Russian iron ore, copper and gold operations. The proven design provides reliable continuous operation in harsh Siberian conditions. Anranst supplies MSHR liner plates, lifter bars and grate panels in Cr20/Cr26 high-chrome iron with Russian-standard compliance.
