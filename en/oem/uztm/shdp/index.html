ЩДП Jaw Crushers

UZTM (УЗТМ) ShDP jaw crushers are the standard primary crushing equipment for Russian mining operations. The double-toggle design provides a powerful crushing stroke ideal for hard iron ore and copper ore. ShDP models from ShDP 9×12 to ShDP 15×21 handle throughputs up to 800 tph. Anranst provides jaw plates in Mn13Cr2 and Mn18Cr2 with Russian-standard compliance and OEM-compatible dimensions.
