KKД Gyratory Crushers

UZTM (УЗТМ) KKD gyratory crushers are Russia's largest primary crushing equipment, manufactured by Uralmash. KKD models with feed openings up to 1,500 mm process Russian iron ore and copper at rates up to 8,000 tph. The heavy-duty design handles the extreme conditions of Siberian mining operations. Anranst provides mantles and concave segments in Mn18Cr2 and Mn22Cr2Mo with Russian-standard material compliance.
