MMD Sizer Teeth & Segments

MMD Mineral Sizers are the world's leading twin-shaft sizers, utilizing the unique principle of sizing by shear and tensile forces rather than compression. This produces less fines and better product shape. MMD sizers handle materials from coal to hard rock, with throughputs up to 10,000 tph. Anranst provides MMD tooth picks in both ZG30CrMnMo alloy steel and ASTM A128 Grade B-2 high manganese steel with verified chemical composition.
