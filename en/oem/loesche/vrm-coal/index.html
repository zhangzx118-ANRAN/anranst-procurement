Loesche

Coal VRM

Loesche vertical roller mills are the global standard for coal grinding in power plants and cement kilns. The modular 2+2 or 3+3 roller configuration provides efficient grinding with low specific energy consumption. The integrated LSKS classifier achieves precise fineness control for different coal types. Anranst supplies grinding rollers, table liners and classifier vanes in Cr26 high-chrome iron with ceramic composite options.
