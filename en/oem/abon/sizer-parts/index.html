ABON Sizer Wear Parts

ABON low-speed sizers are specialized for primary and secondary crushing of coal, salt and soft minerals. The twin-shaft design with large diameter crushing rolls processes material with minimal fines generation. ABON sizers feature bolt-on tooth segments for quick maintenance. Anranst provides grade-specific tooth picks in three weight classes (6.6Kg, 8.6Kg, 25Kg) with ZG30CrMnMo and ZG30CrNiMo alloy options for different crushing duties.

Why Choose Anranst

  • Three-grade tooth system: 6.6kg (ZG30CrMnMo) for secondary crushing, 8.6kg (ZG30CrMnMo) for primary, 25kg (ZG30CrNiMo) for heavy-duty primary with 67% higher toughness
  • ZG30CrNiMo for heavy 25kg tooth picks: Ni substitution increases AKV to 20-25J (vs 10-15J for ZG30CrMnMo) to prevent breakage under higher bending moment
  • Full ABON coverage: 5/130 CCLK, 5/180 CC, 5/180 CC-LK, 5/180 CHD, and larger models

Maintenance Recommendations

Features Routine service requirements are minimal and are limited to checks of frame, tooth and liner bolt tensions, breaker plate adjustments and lubrication checks. When replacement of breaking elements (teeth and breaker plates) is required, this can be carried out in-situ with top feed chute removed or with Sizer withdrawn to the maintenance position.
