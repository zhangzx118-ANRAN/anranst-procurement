HMF Hammer Mills

Hazemag HMF hammer mills are designed for fine crushing of medium-hard to soft materials. The robust rotor with swing hammers achieves high reduction ratios in a single pass. HMF models are widely used in coal, salt and mineral processing applications. Anranst provides hammers in Mn18Cr2 and Cr20 high-chrome iron with optimized wear profiles.
