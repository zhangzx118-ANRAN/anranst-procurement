HSI Impact Crushers

Hazemag HSI (Horizontal Shaft Impact) crushers are world-renowned for their versatility in primary and secondary crushing. The patented SKR rotor design provides optimal blow bar utilization and rapid changeover. Models from APKV to APKV-MH handle capacities up to 2,500 tph in recycling, mining and aggregate applications. Anranst supplies blow bars in Mn18Cr2 work-hardening steel and Cr26 high-chrome iron for different feed conditions.
