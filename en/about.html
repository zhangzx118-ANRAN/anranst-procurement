Advantages

Four Pillars of Competitive Advantage

MMC-Insert Technology

Ceramic particle honeycomb inlay metallurgically bonded to high-Cr, high-Mn or alloy steel matrices. Hardness gradients from HV600 to HV2100. Service life 2-4× conventional castings.
